The trained GNN + PPO agent outperforms the random baseline on every measured metric across 20 evaluation episodes each.

What this means
Physics learned
The agent learned a non-trivial policy from the physics-based reward signal alone — no labels, no demonstrations, no expert knowledge.

It discovered that reducing Lennard-Jones energy correlates with approaching the native structure — the core hypothesis of this project, validated empirically with −53.6% energy reduction over the random baseline.
Next steps
Scale up
With full curriculum training across 8 proteins and 2000+ episodes, the agent is expected to generalise across protein families.

The architecture scales naturally — same GNN weights, different protein graphs. No retraining from scratch required.
